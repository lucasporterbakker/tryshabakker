ABOUT

Trysha Bakker is an award-winning costume designer. After graduating in Fashion Design, her love of film drove her to pursue Costume. She has now been designing for film and television for 40 years. From Maudie, her most recent period feature starring Sally Hawkins and Ethan Hawke, to contemporary work in the forthcoming HBO series Run with Merritt Weaver and Domhnall Gleeson; Trysha's portfolio spans genres as she navigates the past, present, and future.
